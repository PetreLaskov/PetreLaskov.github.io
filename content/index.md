compiled from notes, essays, projects, and sources I find worth integrating.
It starts small and grows by accretion: each new idea becomes a page, each
page links to the others it depends on, and the graph at the edge of this
site is the map of what I'm actually thinking about.

## How this site is organized

Pages carry frontmatter with a `thesis` (the compressed claim), a `status`
(🌱 stub → 🌿 developing → 🌳 mature → 🌲 evergreen), and a `domain`. The
explorer on the left and the graph on the right let you navigate by
structure or by relation. Links between pages matter more than categories —
domains organize, tags slice, links connect.

Everything here is a work in progress.
